## Boundary Contract

## When to Use

- Drafting blog posts, whitepapers, social posts, or landing pages with ChatGPT,
  Claude, Gemini, or any LLM
- Reviewing content from a freelance writer who used AI assistance (assume yes)
- Auditing already-published content for AI-introduced errors


See `procedures/marketing-ai-content-review.md` for detailed rules, examples, and extended reference.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Publishing AI-generated domain claims without expert review | Every technical claim must be reviewed by the domain agent (Graeme for geotechnical) before publication | <!-- hook: allow -->
| Using AI-generated statistics without citing the source | Never publish a statistic that cannot be traced to a verifiable primary source |
| Conflating content review with copy editing | Technical accuracy review (domain agent) and style review (marketing) are separate gates � both are required |
