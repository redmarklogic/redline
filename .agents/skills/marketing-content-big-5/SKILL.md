## Boundary Contract

## When to Use

- Drafting or refreshing the editorial calendar
- A sales rep reports the same prospect question for the third time
- A competitor publishes content you don't have an answer to
- Audience metrics show traffic but no conversion — usually a Big 5 gap


See `procedures/marketing-content-big-5.md` for detailed rules, examples, and extended reference.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Creating content about what the company does instead of what prospects ask | Use Big 5 topics (cost, problems, comparisons, best lists, reviews); company-centric content is not inbound |
| Avoiding the pricing question because it feels risky | Publishing pricing content, even ranges, builds trust and attracts qualified leads |
| Writing for one persona when multiple personas research the same purchase | Create a version for each key persona; a single piece rarely satisfies all decision-makers |
