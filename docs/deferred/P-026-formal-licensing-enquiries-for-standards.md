# Formal licensing enquiries for Standards Knowledge Store internal use

## Why deferred

Architecture decision (ADR-006) establishes citation-only, internal-only as the design principle. But whether internal use of clause references and applicability mappings derived from NZS/AS content requires a formal licence from Standards NZ / Standards Australia has not been verified. NZGS and ACENZ terms are also unverified. No standards content may enter a production system until written responses are obtained. See Graeme's advisory at `docs/knowledge/geotechnical/standards-and-codes/nz-au-standards-ip-classification.md`.

## Unfreeze condition

Written responses obtained from all four bodies (Standards NZ, Standards Australia, NZGS, ACENZ) on software tooling licensing, AND written IP legal opinion under NZ Copyright Act 1994.
