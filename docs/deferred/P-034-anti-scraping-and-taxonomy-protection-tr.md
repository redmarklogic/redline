# Anti-scraping and taxonomy protection — triple approach

## Why deferred

Founder committed to all three layers: (1) Terms of Service prohibition on systematic scraping of taxonomy, rules, or standards-derived content; (2) future technical detection mechanism (rate limiting, fingerprinting, or anomaly detection — implementation deferred); (3) anomalous upload pattern detection (flags users uploading documents that appear designed to extract taxonomy structure rather than review real engineering documents). All three layers are committed in principle. Implementation is deferred to post-launch.

## Unfreeze condition

Skeleton Generator is live and receiving real traffic (post-M1). Layer 1 (ToS) ships with the product. Layers 2–3 are engineering backlog items triggered by first evidence of scraping behaviour or at Sprint 3, whichever comes first.

Founder (policy); Engineering (implementation).
