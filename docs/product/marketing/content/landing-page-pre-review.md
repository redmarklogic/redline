## Headline

**Catch what your reviewers catch -- before they have to.**

## Subhead

Your senior engineers spend hours correcting errors that should never reach their desk.
Wrong standard references. Missing sections. Copy-paste leftovers from the last project.
Pre-Review flags these problems at the point of writing -- so your reviewers can focus
on engineering judgement, not error correction.

## Benefit Bullets

**Standards that stay current.**
`[GRAEME: verify]` Pre-Review checks referenced standards against their current edition
and flags superseded references before the report leaves the author's desk. No more
reviewers hunting for outdated AS or NZS citations.

**Sections that match the brief.**
`[GRAEME: verify]` Reports built from structured templates include all required sections
for the project type. Missing sections are flagged before review, not discovered during
it.

**Content that belongs to this project.**
`[GRAEME: verify]` Copy-pasted sections inherited from previous projects -- wrong site
names, wrong client references, wrong jurisdictional context -- are flagged at the point
of editing, not at the point of final review.

**Reviewer time back where it matters.**
When basic structural and reference errors are caught upstream, your Technical Directors
and Principal Engineers spend their review time on what only they can do: assessing the
engineering content, the interpretive judgements, and the adequacy of the recommendations.
